Teenagers and E-cigarettes

What is Vaping?

Vaping is the act of inhaling and exhaling the aerosol often referred to as vapor produced by an e-cigarette or a similar device. It is called vaping because e-cigarettes do not produce tobacco smoke but an aerosol often mistaken for wtaer vapour that actually contains fine particles. Many of these particles contain varying amounts of toxic chemicals, which have been linked to heart and respiratory diseases and cancer.
